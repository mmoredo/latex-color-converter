Convert HEX and RGB color codes to LaTeX

Easily convert your hex or rgb color to LaTeX's xcolor format.

The xcolor latex package takes rgb colors as parameters following this template: \definecolor{colorname}{rgb}{red, green, blue} where red, green and blue are floating point numbers from 0 to 1. This app lets you convert from this template to standard rgb or hex codes and viceversa.

Get started

Hex must be be six digits between 0 and F and RGB between 0 and 250 separated by a comma.
