How it works

The xcolor latex package takes RGB colors as parameters following this template:

\definecolor{colorname}{rgb}{red, green, blue}

Red, green and blue are floating point numbers from 0 to 1. This app lets you convert from this template to standard RGB or HEX codes.

Don't forget to give your color a unique and identifiable name!

\definecolor{bluish}
